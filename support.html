Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Next Afield's services? +

Getting started is easy! You can contact our sales team through phone, email, or our website contact form. We'll schedule a consultation to understand your agricultural needs and recommend the best solutions for your farm or agribusiness.

What areas do you serve? +

We currently serve agricultural operations across India, with regional offices in major farming states. Our precision agriculture and consulting services are available nationwide, while some on-site services may have geographic limitations. Contact us to confirm availability in your area.

How accurate are your price predictions? +

Our price predictions use advanced machine learning algorithms that analyze historical trends, weather patterns, global market movements, and supply-demand dynamics. While no prediction can be 100% accurate, our system typically achieves 80-90% accuracy, especially as harvest season approaches.

What training do you provide for your technologies? +

We offer comprehensive training for all our technologies and solutions, including:

  • On-site training sessions at your farm
  • Online webinars and video tutorials
  • User manuals and documentation
  • Ongoing support through our help desk
Training is always included with our service packages.

How do I interpret my farm mapping data? +

Our farm mapping data comes with a detailed analysis report and legend explaining all metrics. We also provide a consultation with our precision agriculture specialists to walk you through the findings and recommended actions. Common metrics include soil composition, moisture levels, nutrient distribution, and crop health indicators.
